Samrat Restaurant at Kingsway Camp, Delhi

In the city known primarily for its food, Samrat Restaurant at Kingsway Camp, Delhi is an easy to locate joint where the culinary magic of the chefs is sure to impress every food enthusiast. It is situated in one of the prime areas of the city, Kingsway Camp, that is convenient when it comes to traveling as it is well connected to all other areas of the city. One has easy access to several means of transportation. Fairly priced, the restaurant allows one to afford and enjoy a satiating meal.
